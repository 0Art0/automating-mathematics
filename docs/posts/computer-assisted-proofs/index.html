Computer Assisted Proofs

  • Oct 3, 2019
  • 1 min read

A rapidly growing area is Computer Assisted Proofs of claims that are expressed in precise mathematical terms. These can include ordinary mathematical theorems,as well as claims that pieces of hardware or software, network protocols, and mechanical and hybrid systems meet their specifications. Computer assistance here means both assistance in discovery of proofs as well as checking correctness. Indeed the two aspects are generally integrated in interactive proof systems, with computers filling in the details of proof outlines.
